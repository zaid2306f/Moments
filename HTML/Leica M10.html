The M10 offers dedicated dials for shutter speed, aperture, and ISO settings, allowing photographers to quickly make adjustments without diving into complex menus. 6. **Live View and EVF**: Unlike previous M models, the M10 features live view and an optional electronic viewfinder (EVF), providing a more versatile shooting experience. This addition allows for precise framing and focusing when the rangefinder might be challenging to use. 7. **Wireless Connectivity**: Leica incorporated wireless connectivity in the M10, making it easier to transfer images and control the camera remotely using a smartphone or tablet, enhancing its functionality in the digital age. 8. **Build Quality**: The Leica M10 is built to last, with a robust construction that ensures durability.
