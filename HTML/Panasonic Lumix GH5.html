Panasonic Lumix GH5

The Panasonic Lumix GH5 is a highly regarded mirrorless camera known for its exceptional video capabilities and versatile shooting features. Launched in 2017, it quickly gained popularity among both amateur and professional videographers and photographers. One of its standout features is its 4K video recording at up to 60 frames per second, which, coupled with its advanced image stabilization, makes it a top choice for filmmakers seeking smooth and high-quality footage. The GH5 boasts a robust and weather-sealed magnesium alloy body, making it a durable choice for outdoor shooting in challenging conditions. Its 20.3-megapixel Micro Four Thirds sensor delivers excellent image quality with a wide dynamic range, allowing for detailed and vibrant photos even in low light situations. The camera also offers impressive autofocus capabilities, thanks to its Depth from Defocus (DFD) technology, ensuring quick and accurate subject tracking. Professional videographers appreciate the GH5's extensive video features, including support for 10-bit 4:2:2 color sampling and V-LogL for enhanced color grading options. The camera offers various recording formats, frame rates, and bitrates, allowing for a high degree of customization. Furthermore, its dual SD card slots enable long recording times and redundancy for added security.
